Bad Bunny

Bad Bunny, born Benito Antonio Martinez Ocasio in 1994, is a popular Puerto Rican singer and rapper. He's known for mixing reggaeton and Latin trap in his music. Songs like "Soy Peor" and albums like "X 100pre" and "YHLQMDLG" have made him a big name globally. Besides music, he's into acting and fashion. Bad Bunny isn't just about entertainment; he also cares about social issues, making him more than just a music star – he's someone who wants positive change.
